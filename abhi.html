Advantages of Our Oils

Our oils are known for their purity and numerous health benefits. Here are some important points about each type of oil:

Coconut Oil

Coconut oil is produced from coconuts grown on our own land at the mill, ensuring exceptional quality. It is rich in medium-chain fatty acids, which are beneficial for heart health. Coconut oil is also widely used for cooking, skincare, and hair care due to its moisturizing properties and pleasant aroma.

Groundnut Oil

Sourced from the village of Ranibennur, our groundnut oil is known for its high smoke point, making it ideal for frying and sautéing. It is rich in monounsaturated fats, which help to lower bad cholesterol levels. Groundnut oil also contains antioxidants that protect the body from harmful free radicals.

Sunflower Oil

Our sunflower oil is also sourced from Ranibennur and is prized for its light taste and high vitamin E content. It is an excellent choice for salad dressings and cooking at medium temperatures. Sunflower oil supports skin health and boosts the immune system due to its antioxidant properties.

Sesame Oil (Yall Yanne)

Sesame oil, or Yall Yanne, is highly valued for its nutty flavor and numerous health benefits. is rich in polyunsaturated fats, which are beneficial for cardiovascular health. Sesame oil also contains sesamin and sesamol, which have antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties. It is commonly used in Asian cuisine and for therapeutic massages.
